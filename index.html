Jeffrey Scheduler

Schedule your event in the easiest way possible

Create your event in three easy steps


1. Create
Enter details to tell everyone your time choices. Allow users to propose time choices(or don't, you choose). Add details, choose times, and finish!
2. Survey
Find times where everyone is avaliable and see who's coming. Jeffrey Scheduler makes it easy for your guestswith our intuitive interface, allowing each guest to select what times they can come, and easy for you, with details on which time suits everyone best.
3. Confirm
After confirming a date, change your page to tell users what time you've decided. At this time, users confirm wether or not they can come. Scheduler even makes it easy to collect details, like their choice of meal, or if they can't come the whole time with our simple form interface.
